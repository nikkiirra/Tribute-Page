Shaira Luna

THE "PROMIL GIFTED CHILD" BACK IN THE 90S

WHO IS SHAIRA?

Shaira Luna, considered a “gifted child” and one of the “Promil kids,” grew up living
according to the expectations of people around her. She was a child genius after all.
So it was understandably not easy when she decided to drop out of college.

People thought she would become a doctor before she turned 20—she thought so,
too— but she dropped out of college, picked up a camera, and went on to become
one of today’s most prolific photographers in the country.

“It took a lot of courage to step back because all eyes were on me,” she shared, but
she knew “it wasn’t the path I wanted to get on.”

After shifting to different courses, she left De La Salle University to finally pursue
photography.
