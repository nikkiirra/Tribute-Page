Shaira Luna

THE "PROMIL GIFTED CHILD" BACK IN THE 90S

WHO IS SHAIRA?

Shaira Luna, considered a “gifted child” and one of the “Promil kids,” grew up living according to the expectations of people around her. She was a child genius after all. So it was understandably not easy when she decided to drop out of college. People thought she would become a doctor before she turned 20—she thought so, too— but she dropped out of college, picked up a camera, and went on to become one of today’s most prolific photographers in the country. “It took a lot of courage to step back because all eyes were on me,” she shared, but she knew “it wasn’t the path I wanted to get on.” After shifting to different courses, she left De La Salle University to finally pursue photography.

WHY SHAIRA LUNA?

Why Shaira Luna? Why not other professional photographers known internationally? I look up to Shaira because she has the strength to stand up and reach for her own dreams. She’s an intelligent kid, people expected and wanted her to become a doctor. But she chose photography. I admired her bravery and perseverance despite the doubters of her chosen field. Not only that, the artistry in her craft is truly innate in her as if she had been doing this her whole life. She may be known as the 90’s brightest kid but Shaira has eyes for art and photography. That’s why it’s Shaira for me.
